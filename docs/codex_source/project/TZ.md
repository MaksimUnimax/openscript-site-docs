OpenScript / AI Starter Community is a single website product. Courses are not a separate root product; they live inside the "Работа с ИИ" materials area in the main cabinet.

Product scope:
- public landing page
- registration
- email verification
- login and logout
- password reset
- personal cabinet
- "Работа с ИИ" materials section
- tariffs and paid options as product concepts
- admin area
- future payments and access logic
- future AI-sales agent flows

Auth scope:
- login remains email OR login
- public registration may be temporarily closed when the product is not ready for new signups
- email verification remains available for already issued tokens
- password reset remains available through email

Materials and course scope:
- the materials section is the place for future courses, lessons, tasks, and study materials
- course content must feel beginner-friendly, visual, and practical
- do not present fake finished access, ownership, or payment claims before the access model is ready
- course content should be framed as a learning path inside the cabinet, not a separate product

Business scope:
- tariffs and paid options are part of the planned product structure
- future payments may later unlock materials or course access
- admin tools may later manage content and access rules
- the AI-sales agent is a future product capability, not part of the first course shell

Current state assumption:
- the app already has a cabinet shell and a materials placeholder
- the docs layer is being repaired/imported before course generation work begins
