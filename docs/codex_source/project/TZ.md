# TZ — OpenScript / AI Starter Community

## 1. Essence

Create and maintain the main OpenScript website and personal cabinet for people without programming experience who want to start making simple programs, bots, scripts, automations and MVPs with AI tools.

The system includes:
- landing;
- registration;
- email verification;
- login/logout;
- password reset;
- cabinet;
- tariffs;
- paid options;
- “Работа с ИИ” materials;
- courses inside paid materials area;
- admin;
- email notifications;
- future payments;
- future AI-sales agent.

## 2. MVP goal

The user can:

1. Open the landing page.
2. Register.
3. Confirm email.
4. Log into cabinet.
5. See access status.
6. See starter tariff.
7. Pay starter tariff in future payment stage.
8. Receive permanent access to materials after first successful payment.
9. See active paid options and expiration dates.
10. Extend paid options in future payment stage.
11. Open “Работа с ИИ” materials/courses after access is granted.

Admin can:

1. Manage users.
2. Manage roles.
3. Manage tariffs.
4. Manage paid options.
5. See access/payment status.
6. Manage future payment/admin functions.

## 3. Positioning

The product helps people start making simple programs without knowing code.

External messaging must be simple:
- no heavy AI/IT jargon on first screen;
- no promises of becoming senior developer;
- no guaranteed income/job;
- no “we build everything for you” claim.

## 4. Access model

Registration happens before payment.

First successful payment of any starter tariff grants permanent access to materials.

Paid options are separate from materials access:
- AI tool / GPT;
- server;
- VPN;
- future mentor/support/options.

If a user stops extending options, materials access remains.

## 5. Courses/materials

Courses are inside the main product:
- in personal cabinet;
- after payment/access;
- under “Работа с ИИ”.

They are not the root product.
They support the main promise.
